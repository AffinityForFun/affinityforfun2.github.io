About Me

My name is Justin. I have been a hobbyist game developer since 2010, with 5+ years of education and work experience. Graduating in summer 2018, I hope to pursue a career in the games industry.

Game development has come to have a significant influence in my life, serving as my main creative outlet and career of choice. I have worked on countless projects, focused on exploring the creative space of game design and growing my skills as a developer. I have included a selection of these in my portfolio. I am very grateful to be a part of such an energetic and creatively rewarding field.

Currently, I am serving as VP of Operations for Husky Game Development at Michigan Technological University where I oversee 60+ students in general meetings, where I speak on topics ranging from game design to technical implementation. I also oversee two teams as the work to produce year long game design projects.

I am also working on my Senior game design project, serving as Lead Programmer and Co-Lead Designer. It is a 3D-Puzzle game, challenging the player to use 3D, 2D, and 1D spatial reasoning skills to solve puzzles that cross the spatial dimensions. It is planned to be brought to market at the end of Summer 2018.
